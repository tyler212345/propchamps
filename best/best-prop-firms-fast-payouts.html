How we ranked

Speed, backed by proof.

A fast median payout is only meaningful if the firm has actually paid a lot of traders. So we ranked on two axes: the median time from request to money received, and the total amount paid out — a quick time on a tiny track record doesn't earn the top spot. We also note which firms offer daily payouts. All figures come from our payouts tracker and are refreshed weekly.

Frequently asked

Fast payout questions.

Which prop firm pays out the fastest? +
Apex Trader Funding, at around a 4-hour median on the largest total paid ($300M+). My Funded Futures is next (~6 hours) with a daily-payout path. See the data on the payouts tracker.
What counts as a fast payout? +
Same-day to a couple of days is strong; weekly or every-five-trading-days is the normal cadence. The number that matters is the median time from request to money received, plus a large, verifiable history behind it.
Do fast payouts mean a firm is reliable? +
Read speed and reliability together. A quick median backed by a large total paid — like Apex's $300M+ or My Funded Futures' $80M+ — is more trustworthy than a fast time on a tiny record.
Which prop firms offer daily payouts? +
My Funded Futures (Rapid product) and Tradeify (Select Daily path) offer daily payouts. Most other firms pay on a fixed cadence such as every five trading days.
