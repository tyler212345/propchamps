Frequently asked

Cheap challenge questions.

Which prop firm challenge is cheapest? +
With CHAMP, Apex Trader Funding is the cheapest to start — 90% off plus no activation fee. Top One (60% off) and Lucid (50% off + free activation) follow. Confirm the final price on the deals page.
Does the discount cover the activation fee? +
Depends on the firm. Apex waives activation with CHAMP, and My Funded Futures and Lucid have no activation fee at all. Others charge a one-time activation at funding, so factor it into the true cost.
Is a cheaper prop firm worse? +
Not necessarily — Apex is both the cheapest and the most proven by total payouts. But always check the rules and payout record too; a cheap challenge with slow payouts is a false economy. Use the payouts tracker.
How do I get the CHAMP discount? +
Enter CHAMP at checkout on the firm's site. We re-verify it weekly across account sizes; if it stops working at any tier it's pulled within 24 hours.
