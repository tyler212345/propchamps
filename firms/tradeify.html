FAQ

Common Questions

What is the difference between Growth and Select at Tradeify?
Growth has a DLL during eval but no consistency requirement. Select removes the DLL during eval but requires 40% consistency. After passing Select, you choose between a daily or flex payout path.
Does Tradeify offer daily payouts?
Yes — the Select Daily funding path offers daily payouts with per-day payout caps of $600 to $2,500 depending on account size.
Does Tradeify have a DLL?
It depends on the plan. Growth eval has a DLL. Select eval has no DLL. Select Flex funded also has no DLL. Lightning removes DLL on the 25K account only.
What platforms does Tradeify support?
Tradovate and WealthCharts across all plans.
